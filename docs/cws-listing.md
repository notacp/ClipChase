## Full description

```
ClipChase is Ctrl+F for YouTube. Type a word or phrase, and it searches every transcript on a channel. You get every video that mentions it, with clickable timestamps that jump to the exact second.

Hunting a clip for an edit or a compilation? You remember the line but not the video? Search what they said and land on the moment. No scrubbing through hour-long videos.

What it does:
• YouTube transcript search across a whole channel, not one video at a time
• Find the exact moment in a video: every result is a timestamp, one click from the second it was said
• Clip finder for editors and fans: get every time a catchphrase or line was said, sorted by how often it comes up
• Timestamp search with time-range filters: 1 day, 7 days, 1 month, 6 months, or all time
• Works in हिंदी, English, and 100+ languages. If the video has captions, it's searchable
• Free, no account needed

How to use it:
1. Open any YouTube channel, or paste its name into the extension
2. Search a word or phrase
3. Click a timestamp. The video opens at that exact moment

Made for clip hunters, fan editors, and meme makers who need the source of a moment fast. Also handy for writers, students, and anyone who needs an exact quote with a timestamp.

Search a YouTube channel the way you search a document. Install ClipChase and find the moment.
```
